Pre-Primary education is considered to be fundamental to the development of the human nature.
It contributes to the development of the child in all areas. cognitive, emotional, social, moral, psychomotor, aesthetic.
It also contributes to the acquisition of skills useful for their lives, right attitudes and values.
In addition, the following aims are persuaded through a variety of educational programmes:
Development of creative expression and thinking.
Pupils’ preparation for primary school and maximizing their potential for school success.
Development of attributes of their personality, such as taking initiatives, persistence, self-confidence, optimism.
